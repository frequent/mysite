Contact

Do you have any questions or ideas? Speaker requests to take part in events? Let me know! You can contact me at hello@svenfranck.eu or on my Volt email sven.franck@volteuropa.org.

For general questions on how to join Volt or how to support us, please contact info@volteuropa.org for Volt Europa or info@voltfrance.org for our french chapter.

You can of course find me on the social networks, but don't hesitate to contact me directly.I look forward to hearing from you!
